## BENCHMARK COMPARISON LOGIC

1. Fetch benchmark historical prices for selected range
   (same Yahoo Finance endpoint as portfolio chart)

2. Normalize both series to 100 at the start of the period:
   normalizedValue = (currentPrice / startPrice) × 100

3. Plot two lines on same chart:
   - Portfolio line: gold (#C9A84C)
   - Benchmark line: white/grey (#8A9BB0), dashed

4. Show legend:
   [● Your Portfolio]  [- - S&P 500]

5. Show summary below chart:
   Your Portfolio:  +XX.XX%
   S&P 500:         +XX.XX%
   Difference:      +X.XX% (outperforming / underperforming)
